## Description
```
Zdorovie is a modern, open-source website for medical clinics.

Patients describe their symptoms in plain language and get matched to the right specialist,
then book online or via Telegram in about two minutes. The clinic gets simple dashboards for
doctors and admins, a patient medical card, and automated Telegram + voice confirmations.

It's fully bilingual (EN/RU) — the language switch translates not just the interface but also
the database content: doctor names, specialties, descriptions, services, prices and durations.

Built with plain HTML/CSS/JS on top of Supabase (PostgreSQL + Edge Functions), so it loads
instantly, hosts free on Vercel/GitHub Pages, and is easy to self-host. MIT licensed.

Highlights:
• AI symptom-to-doctor matching
• Online + Telegram booking with reminders
• Patient / doctor / admin dashboards
• 20+ doctors, 100+ services with live search
• Light/dark theme, responsive, SEO-ready
• 100% open source (MIT)

Feedback very welcome — especially on the booking flow and the AI matching!
```

I built Zdorovie because picking the right doctor is confusing and small clinics can't afford
